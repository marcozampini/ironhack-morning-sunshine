Today we speak about Personal Bloom!

Personal Bloom vs Personal Development

Personal Development

Personal development consists of activities that develop a person's capabilities and potential, build human capital, facilitate employability, and enhance quality of life and the realization of dreams and aspirations

Synonims of Personal Development

  • Personal Growth
  • Self-Improvement
  • Self-Growth

When we speak about personal development we often use the way of thinking of the spirit of this time.

You must run, you must improve yourself, you must be productive, you must do, you must demonstrate that you have changed.

And this brings a lot of anxieties and often leads to the balance sheets of their year and their history as is done with company turnover

and therefore with the idea that one must constantly accumulate and increase one's inner turnover

While this can be good for a company, it's not good for one person.

Personal growth is often a set of techniques that are applied to a person who, however, has something unique, to which something standard cannot be applied

for this reason maura gancitano and andrea colamedici have recovered the idea of personal flowering / personal flourishing / personal bloom

The expression "Personal bloom" comes from Ancient Greek practices of epimeleia heautou, or techniques of “care of the self”

Personal bloom is self-care, while personal development is work on oneself, that implies effort, a goal to achieve, a constant anxiety that the society of performance constantly pushes us to have

A personal bloom process is a process in which a person feels himself/herself in motion, the person feels that he/she is acting but in his/her own way, at their own pace, according to one's vocation.

It's the sensation to feel more in harmony with yourself and your desires

those who practice personal flowering want a process that can be individual or collective that has to do with listening to oneself

it is a process of care and curiosity (cura e curiosità have the same etymological root), care about themselves, of their own spaces, of their own time, and also be curious, that is feeling in motion.

Our brain doesn't need to mull over things, it needs to act, the human being is made to act, but everyone needs to act in a different way, according to their peculiarity and one's vocation/

The process of personal bloom helps to feel in harmony, to feel free to rediscover yourself, your pace and therefore to find your direction
